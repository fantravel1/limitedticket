Common Questions

Ticket Intelligence FAQ

Direct answers from real fan experiences and industry knowledge.

How do I get tickets when they sell out instantly?

Register for every official presale: Verified Fan, artist presales, venue presales, and credit card presales. Set up accounts and payment methods days before. On sale day, have multiple devices ready, log in early, and never refresh once you're in a queue. Official waitlists and platform resale are your safest second chances.

When do tickets go on sale and what time zone?

It varies by event and region. Most US concerts drop at 10 AM local venue time, UK events at 9-10 AM GMT. Our Drop Calendar tracks every date globally with automatic time zone conversion so you always know the exact moment in your local time.

What is Verified Fan and how does it work?

Ticketmaster's Verified Fan filters real fans from bots. Register during the open window (1-2 weeks before sale), verify your identity, and wait for a code. If selected, you get a unique access code and purchase window. Being waitlisted isn't a dead end — codes are released in waves as selected fans don't complete purchases.

How do I avoid getting scammed on resale tickets?

Only buy from official platforms or authorized resale marketplaces. Never buy through social media DMs or random websites. Use payment methods with buyer protection. If the price seems too good to be true, it is. Our Scam Shield has event-specific warnings and platform safety ratings.

Why are tickets so hard to get in the first place?

A stadium holds 60,000 but millions want to attend. Bots capture significant inventory despite defenses. Limited tour dates concentrate demand. Presale allocations reduce general sale inventory. Dynamic pricing raises costs as demand spikes. Our LimitedScore rates each event 1-100 so you know what you're facing.

What if I missed the ticket drop entirely?

Join the official waitlist immediately. Check official resale — tickets get listed as plans change. Monitor for added show dates (high demand often leads to more performances). Verified resale platforms often see price drops 24-48 hours before events. Consider less popular cities on the same tour.
